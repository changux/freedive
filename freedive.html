Create user searchable databases in minutes

Transform Google Spreadsheets into embeddable, searchable databases.


Key Features

  • Easily search your data.
  • Get results in an interactive, sortable table
  • Use filters to refine the search.
  • A wizard walks you through creating a widget.
  • Customize the interface.
  • Share search results on social networks.

Technical stuff
